Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Our Commitment: BeardVPN is committed to protecting your privacy. We operate under a strict no-logs policy and collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address (for account recovery and support)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Google Play; we do not store payment details

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or website visits
  • DNS queries
  • IP addresses associated with your activity
  • Connection timestamps
  • Session duration
  • Traffic data or content

1.3 Technical Data

  • App Analytics: Anonymous crash reports and app performance data (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version (for compatibility purposes only)

2. No-Logs Policy

BeardVPN maintains a strict no-logs policy. We do not monitor, record, log, store, or share any of your online activities while connected to our VPN service. This includes:

  • No logging of browsing activity
  • No logging of connection timestamps
  • No logging of session information
  • No logging of IP addresses
  • No logging of DNS queries

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining our VPN service
  • Processing subscription payments through Google Play
  • Sending important service notifications
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Responding to customer support requests

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Google Play for payment processing, Firebase for analytics
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, though our no-logs policy means we have minimal data to provide

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • WireGuard protocol with state-of-the-art encryption
  • Secure key storage on your device
  • No centralized user activity logs

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics (in app settings)
  • Cancel your subscription at any time

7. Children's Privacy

BeardVPN is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@beardapps.com
